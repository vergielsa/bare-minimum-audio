About the Business

At Bare Minimum Audio, we make your local dive bar show sound like a headline set. You bring the instruments — we bring the gear, the experience, and everything it takes to turn it up to 11.

From small bar stages to outdoor festival rigs, we handle sound and lighting for bands and events of all sizes across the Madison area. No fuss, no drama — just great sound and a crew that knows what they're doing.
